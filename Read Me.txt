# Retail Customer Analytics & Revenue Intelligence

## Project Overview

This project analyzes an online retail dataset to uncover customer purchasing behavior, revenue trends, product performance, and cross-selling opportunities. It combines business intelligence, statistical analysis, customer segmentation, unsupervised machine learning, and market basket analysis to generate actionable business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

The project demonstrates an end-to-end analytics workflow including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), customer segmentation using both RFM Analysis and K-Means Clustering, association rule mining using the Apriori algorithm, and an interactive Tableau Executive Dashboard.

---

# Business Problem Statement

Retail businesses generate millions of transactional records but often struggle to answer critical business questions such as:

* Which customers generate the highest revenue?
* Which customers are at risk of churn?
* Which products contribute most to revenue?
* Which products are frequently purchased together?
* How can customers be segmented for targeted marketing?
* Which markets contribute the highest sales?
* How can business executives monitor key performance indicators effectively?

The objective of this project is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence that supports customer retention, revenue growth, personalized marketing, inventory optimization, and cross-selling strategies.

---

# Objectives

* Analyze overall retail sales performance.
* Identify revenue trends over time.
* Evaluate customer purchasing behavior.
* Perform customer segmentation using RFM Analysis.
* Compare RFM Segmentation with K-Means Clustering.
* Discover product purchase associations using Market Basket Analysis.
* Build an executive-level Tableau dashboard.
* Generate business recommendations based on analytical findings.

# Data Cleaning

Performed the following preprocessing steps:

* Removed duplicate transactions
* Removed cancelled invoices
* Removed missing Customer IDs
* Removed negative quantities
* Created Revenue feature
* Converted Invoice Date to datetime
* Created Year-Month feature
* Created RFM dataset
* Standardized data for clustering

---

# Exploratory Data Analysis

Performed analysis on:

* Overall KPIs
* Monthly Revenue Trend
* Country-wise Revenue Analysis
* Top Revenue Products
* Top Customers
* Customer Purchase Distribution
* Revenue Contribution Analysis

---

# RFM Analysis

Calculated:

* **Recency**
* **Frequency**
* **Monetary Value**

Customers were segmented into:

* Champions
* Loyal Customers
* Potential Loyalists
* Need Attention
* At Risk
* Lost Customers

This segmentation supports customer retention and personalized marketing campaigns.

---

# K-Means Customer Clustering

Applied K-Means clustering using standardized RFM features.

### Cluster Validation

* Elbow Method
* Silhouette Score

Final model selected **4 customer clusters**.

Cluster profiling included:

* Average Recency
* Average Frequency
* Average Monetary Value
* Revenue Contribution
* Customer Count

Comparison with RFM segmentation was also performed to evaluate business interpretability.

---

# Market Basket Analysis

Applied Apriori Algorithm to identify frequent product combinations.

Generated:

The analysis identified strong product affinities that can be leveraged for:

* Cross-selling

* Store Layout Optimization
